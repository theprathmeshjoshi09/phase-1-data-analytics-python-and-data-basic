# 🔹 Types of Questions

---

## 1. What is happening?

👉 Understand current situation

Example:

* What are total sales?
* Which product sells most?

---

## 2. Why is it happening?

👉 Find causes

Example:

* Why did sales drop?
* Why is churn increasing?

---

## 🔥 Interview Thinking

* Best analysts always ask WHY

---

## 3. What will happen next?

👉 Predict future

Example:

* Will demand increase?
* Will users churn more?

---

## 4. What should we do?

👉 Recommend action

Example:

* How to improve sales?
* How to reduce churn?

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Best analysis answers all 4 levels
