# 🔹 Why It Matters

* converts data into value

## 📊 Real Insight

# 🔹 What Makes a Good Conclusion?

---

## 1. Clear

* easy to understand
* no confusion

---

## 2. Data-backed

* supported by analysis
* based on evidence

---

## 3. Actionable

* leads to next step
* supports decision

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 If no action → weak conclusion

---

# 🔹 Analyst Thinking

Ask:

* What decision can be made?
* What action should be taken?
* What is the impact?

👉 "What should we do now?"
