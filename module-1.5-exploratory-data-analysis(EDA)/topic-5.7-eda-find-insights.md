# 🔹 What is an Insight?

Insight = Pattern + Meaning

* Pattern → What is happening
* Meaning → Why it is happening + what to do

---

## 📊 Example

❌ Pattern: Sales increased in December

✅ Insight:
Sales increased in December due to holiday demand
→ Opportunity for marketing campaigns

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Without meaning, data is incomplete

# 🔹 How to Find Insights
