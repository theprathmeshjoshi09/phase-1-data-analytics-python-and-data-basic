# 13. Storytelling with Data

Storytelling with data is the process of:
👉 combining data + visuals + narrative
👉 to influence decisions

---

## 📊 Data Analyst Perspective

Used for:

* business presentations
* executive reports
* stakeholder communication

---

# 🔹 13.1 Why Storytelling is Critical

* simplifies complex analysis
* drives action
* improves understanding
* makes insights memorable

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Good analysis + poor storytelling = ignored work

---

# 🔹 13.2 Core Elements of Storytelling

---

## 1. Data

* accurate
* relevant
* clean

---

## 2. Visuals

* clear
* meaningful
* simple

---

## 3. Narrative

* explains insights
* connects dots
* guides audience

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 All three must align

---

# 🔹 13.3 Story Structure (Golden Framework)

---

## 1. Context

👉 What is the problem?

---

## 2. Insight

👉 What did you find?

---

## 3. Impact

👉 Why it matters?

---

## 4. Action

👉 What should be done?

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Always answer:

* What happened
* Why it happened
* What to do next

---

# 🔹 13.4 Example Story

---

## Raw Data

Sales dropped by 25% in April

---

## Weak Communication

👉 “Sales decreased”

---

## Strong Story

👉 “Sales dropped by 25% in April due to supply chain delays.
Improving inventory planning can prevent further loss.”

---

## 📊 Insight

👉 Data + reason + action

---

# 🔹 13.5 Choosing the Right Visual for Story

---

## 📊 Examples

* trend → line chart
* comparison → bar chart
* distribution → histogram

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Visual must support message

---

# 🔹 13.6 Audience-Centric Storytelling

---

## 📊 Example

* Technical audience → detailed
* Business audience → simple

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Tailor story to audience

---

# 🔹 13.7 Highlighting Key Insights

---

## 📊 Techniques

* annotations
* colors
* labels
* callouts

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Guide attention

---

# 🔹 13.8 Avoiding Bad Storytelling

---

## ❌ Mistakes

* too many charts
* no clear message
* overcomplicated visuals
* ignoring context

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Clarity > complexity

---

# 🔹 13.9 Real-World Workflow

---

## 📊 Process

1. Analyze data
2. Identify key insight
3. Choose visualization
4. Build story
5. Suggest action

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Don’t show everything
👉 Show what matters
