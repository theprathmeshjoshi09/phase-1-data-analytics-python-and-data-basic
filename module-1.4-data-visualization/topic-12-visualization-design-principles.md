# 12. Visualization Design Principles

These principles help you create:
👉 clear
👉 accurate
👉 effective visualizations

---

## 📊 Data Analyst Perspective

Used for:

* dashboards
* reports
* presentations
* stakeholder communication

---

# 🔹 12.1 Clarity

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Make charts easy to understand

---

## 📊 Example

* clear labels
* readable axes
* simple titles

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 If user has to think too much → design failed

---

# 🔹 12.2 Simplicity

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Remove unnecessary elements

---

## ❌ Avoid

* too many colors
* excessive labels
* clutter

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Simple charts are more powerful

---

# 🔹 12.3 Consistency

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Keep design uniform

---

## 📊 Examples

* same colors for same category
* consistent font sizes
* uniform layout

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Consistency builds trust

---

# 🔹 12.4 Color Usage

---

## 📊 Guidelines

* use limited colors
* highlight important data
* avoid confusing palettes

---

## 📊 Example

* red → loss
* green → profit

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Color = meaning, not decoration

---

# 🔹 12.5 Proper Chart Selection

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Match chart with data

---

## 📊 Examples

* trend → line chart
* comparison → bar chart
* distribution → histogram

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Wrong chart = wrong message

---

# 🔹 12.6 Avoid Misleading Visuals

---

## ❌ Common Issues

* distorted axes
* truncated scales
* exaggerated visuals

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Ethical responsibility

---

# 🔹 12.7 Focus & Attention

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Highlight key insights

---

## 📊 Techniques

* bold colors
* annotations
* positioning

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Guide user’s eye

---

# 🔹 12.8 Data-to-Ink Ratio

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Show only meaningful data

---

## 📊 Avoid

* unnecessary gridlines
* decorative elements

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Less noise = more clarity

---

# 🔹 12.9 Accessibility

---

## 📊 Consider

* color blindness
* readability
* contrast

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Design for everyone

---

# 🔹 12.10 Story Alignment

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Visualization should support story

---

## 📊 Example

* highlight key trend
* remove distractions

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Chart must answer question

---

# 🔹 12.11 Layout & Hierarchy

---

## 📊 Rule

👉 Organize visuals logically

---

## 📊 Example

* important charts at top
* supporting visuals below

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Layout = flow of understanding

---
