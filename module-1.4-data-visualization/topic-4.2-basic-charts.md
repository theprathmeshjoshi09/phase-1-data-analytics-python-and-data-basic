# 2. Basic Charts

Basic charts are the **foundation of data visualization**. These are the
most commonly used charts in real-world analysis.

---

## 📊 Data Analyst Perspective

Used for:

* trends
* comparisons
* distributions
* relationships

---

# 2.1 Line Chart (Trends Over Time)

Used to show **changes over time**

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

```
Jan: 100
Feb: 120
Mar: 150
Apr: 180
```

👉 Shows upward trend clearly

---

## 📊 Applications

* sales growth
* stock prices
* website traffic

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Best chart for **time-series data**

---

# 🔍 Interview Thinking

* When to use line chart?
  👉 time-based trends

---

# 2.2 Bar Chart (Comparison)

Used to compare values across categories

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

| Product | Sales |
| ------- | ----- |
| A       | 100   |
| B       | 200   |
| C       | 150   |

👉 Easy comparison

---

## 📊 Applications

* product comparison
* category analysis

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Best for comparing categories

---

# 2.3 Stacked Bar Chart

Shows **composition + comparison**

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

| Product | Online | Offline |
| ------- | ------ | ------- |
| A       | 50     | 50      |
| B       | 120    | 80      |

---

## 📊 Applications

* sales breakdown
* contribution analysis

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Shows both:

* total
* parts

---

# 2.4 Horizontal Bar Chart

Same as bar chart but horizontal

---

## 📊 When to Use

* long category names
* better readability

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Improves clarity for text-heavy data

---

# 2.5 Histogram (Distribution)

Shows how data is distributed

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

```
Marks: [10, 20, 20, 30, 40, 50]
```

---

## 📊 Applications

* understanding spread
* detecting skewness

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Best for **continuous data**

---

# 🔍 Interview Thinking

* Histogram vs bar chart?
  👉 distribution vs comparison

---

# 2.6 Scatter Plot (Relationship)

Shows relationship between two variables

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

* Height vs Weight

---

## 📊 Applications

* correlation analysis
* feature relationships

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Helps detect:

* positive correlation
* negative correlation

---

# 🔍 Interview Thinking

* When to use scatter plot?
  👉 relationship between variables

---

# 2.7 Area Chart

Similar to line chart but filled area

---

## 📊 Applications

* cumulative trends
* volume emphasis

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Highlights magnitude

---

# 2.8 Pie Chart (Use Carefully ⚠️)

Shows proportion of categories

---

## 📊 Real Use Case

| Category | %  |
| -------- | -- |
| A        | 40 |
| B        | 30 |
| C        | 30 |

---

## ⚠️ Limitation

👉 Hard to compare precisely

---

## 🔥 Insight

👉 Avoid when many categories

---

# 2.9 Advanced Variations

---

## 🔸 Grouped Bar Chart

Compare multiple categories

👉 Example:

* Sales 2023 vs 2024

---

## 🔸 Dual-Axis Chart

Two variables with different scales

---

## ⚠️ Warning

👉 Can be misleading if used incorrectly

---

## 🔸 Step Plot

Shows step-wise changes

---

## 📊 Applications

* pricing changes
* policy updates

---
