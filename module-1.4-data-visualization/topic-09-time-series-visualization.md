## Topic 09: Data Storytelling

---

# 🔥 Real-World Perspective

In real-world business:

👉 Data alone does NOT drive decisions
👉 Stories do

Managers don’t want:

* raw numbers
* complex charts

They want:
👉 **clear insights + clear message**

---

## 🧠 Real Insight

👉 Data storytelling = turning data into decisions

---

# 9. Data Storytelling

Data storytelling is the process of:
👉 combining data + visuals + narrative
👉 to communicate insights effectively

---

## 📊 Data Analyst Perspective

Used for:

* presentations
* reports
* stakeholder communication

---

# 🔹 9.1 Why Storytelling Matters

* simplifies complex data
* improves decision-making
* makes insights memorable

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Good story = action
👉 Bad story = confusion

---

# 🔹 9.2 Components of Data Storytelling

---

## 1. Data

Accurate and relevant

---

## 2. Visualization

Clear and meaningful

---

## 3. Narrative

Explanation of insights

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 All three must work together

---

# 🔹 9.3 Story Structure

---

## 1. Context

👉 What is the problem?

---

## 2. Insight

👉 What did you find?

---

## 3. Action

👉 What should be done?

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Always answer:

* what
* why
* what next

---

# 🔹 9.4 Example Story

---

## Raw Data

Sales dropped in March

---

## Story

👉 “Sales dropped by 20% in March due to supply issues.
We recommend improving inventory management.”

---

## 📊 Insight

👉 Data → explanation → action

---

# 🔹 9.5 Choosing the Right Visual

---

## 📊 Examples

* Trend → line chart
* Comparison → bar chart
* Distribution → histogram

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Visualization supports story

---

# 🔹 9.6 Avoiding Misleading Stories

---

## ❌ Common Issues

* cherry-picking data
* misleading scales
* ignoring context

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Ethics matter in data storytelling

---

# 🔹 9.7 Audience Awareness

---

## 📊 Example

* Technical audience → detailed
* Business audience → simple

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Story depends on audience

---

# 🔹 9.8 Real-World Workflow

---

## 📊 Process

1. Analyze data
2. Identify key insight
3. Choose visualization
4. Build narrative

---

## 📊 Real Insight

👉 Don’t show everything
👉 Show what matters

---

❌ No clear message
❌ Complex visuals
❌ Ignoring audience

# 💡 Key Takeaways

✔ Always suggest action
